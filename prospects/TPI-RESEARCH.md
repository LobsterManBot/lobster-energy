## What Are TPIs?

Third Party Intermediaries - businesses that help other businesses compare and switch energy suppliers. They earn commission from suppliers when they switch a customer.

**Why they need Lobster Energy:**
- They advise clients on WHEN to buy (timing the market)
- Better market intel = more trust = more clients
- Our signals could be their secret weapon
